Panchshila Co-operative House Building Society Ltd

Panchshila Co-operative House Building Society Ltd was formed in the year 1968 by Late Shri R P Barman along with his four friends. They named the Society “PANCHSHILA” which means five rocks, signifying their friendship.

The colony called the Panchshila Park is astride the Outer Ring Road (ORR), located between the prestigious Delhi IIT in the West and the commercial hub of Nehru Place in the East. To the North it has the massive reserved forest with its jogging and walking tracks, and in the South is the busy shopping centre of Malviya Nagar.
